Makes things happen.

The basic idea of Teamformer reflects that as MOOCs rise, there are many courses, that encourage team work and team projects or even require it.

Surely, you can look up collaboratos in forums, but this lacks transparency, especially if you consider that there are MOOCs to which tens of thousands are enrolled. In addition, collaboration is certainly a part of MOOCs spirit, but search for team mates is a prevalent need.

Ambition of Teamformer is to answer this need in a elegant way.

As Teamformer originated as tool for putting together teams to collaborate on course projects, one part let's you browse ongoing classes and look for projects in a specific MOOC class. If the class you are looking for does not exist, you are free to create it.

The other part is a generic "project vs. profile" marketplace where founders can promote their ideas and attract the interest of people registered in and vice versa.

You can think of Teamformer as a "crowdfunding" platform to raise human capital.

There are two roles Founder and Skiller. They are not exclusive, you can both search for co-founder / team mates and at the same time investigate for project which is challenging and is looking for someone with your know how.

The profiles are shared across all the classes and the generic marketplace as well. The project has to belong to specific class or to the general project pool.

As last, but not least, you can endorse your colleaguess and such way help to form future collaborations in a better way on the basis of reputation.
